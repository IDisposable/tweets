There are 365.2425 days per solar year. That's why the leap year rule is every 4 years (the 0.25 part times 4), EXCEPT every 100 years as the 0.24 is 100th less than 1/4, so skip a year, EXCEPT that every 400 years the 0.0025 part adds a leap day.
